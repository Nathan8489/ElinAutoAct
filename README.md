# Auto Act
Automatically perform the same type of action, triggered by holding down the Shift key and taking the action.

| Supported Actions |
|:------------------:|
|Mine               |
|Collect            |
|Harvest           |
|Shear             |
|Brush             |
|Plow               |
|Plant Seed        |
|Reap Seed         |
|Fertilize         |
|Draw Water         |
|Pour Water         |
|Clean             |
|Read              |
|Pick              |
|Build              |
|Dig               |
|Steal               |
|Pick Lock          |
|Water Crops(Auto refill)|
